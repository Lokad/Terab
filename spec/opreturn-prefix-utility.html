Claim your Bitcoin Protocol Identifier

This page is a largely self-contained JS app that let you validate your claim of an OP_RETURN protocol identifier for Bitcoin against the up-to-date list of protocol identifiers as already claimed on GitHub. The form below validates that your claim is compliant with this guideline. Once you have chosen your identifier, you can retrieve a modified version of protocols.csv and proceeds with a pull request. We will try to diligently process your request. Thank your very much for your contribution to the Bitcoin ecosystem. I hope that you will find success in your project.
